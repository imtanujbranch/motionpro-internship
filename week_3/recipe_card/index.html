Classic Caprese Salad

Italian
★★★★★ (4.8/5)

Fresh mozzarella, ripe tomatoes, and basil drizzled with olive oil and balsamic. Simple Italian perfection.

Ingredients

  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 300g fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

1. Arrange tomato and mozzarella slices alternately on a platter. 2. Tuck basil leaves between slices. 3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar. 4. Season with salt and pepper. 5. Let stand 15 minutes before serving. 6. Serve with crusty bread.

Black Bean Veggie Tacos

Mexican
★★★★☆ (4.5/5)

Hearty black beans with fresh vegetables in soft tortillas. A delicious and nutritious vegetarian meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans black beans, drained
  • 8 soft tortillas
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Lettuce, tomatoes, avocado for serving

Preparation

1. Sauté onion and garlic until soft. 2. Add black beans and spices, cook 5 minutes. 3. Warm tortillas in dry pan. 4. Fill tortillas with bean mixture. 5. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, and avocado. 6. Serve immediately with lime wedges.

Vegetable Stir Fry Bowl

Asian
★★★★★ (4.7/5)

Fresh mixed vegetables stir-fried in savory sauce served over steamed rice. Colorful and nutritious.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, bell peppers)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • Green onions for garnish

Preparation

1. Heat oil in wok over high heat. 2. Add garlic and ginger, stir-fry 30 seconds. 3. Add vegetables and stir-fry 3-4 minutes. 4. Add soy sauce and toss to coat. 5. Serve over rice and garnish with green onions. 6. Sprinkle with sesame seeds if desired.

Plant-Based Veggie Burger

American
★★★★☆ (4.3/5)

Hearty veggie patty made with black beans and vegetables on a toasted bun. Healthy comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1 can black beans, mashed
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced

Preparation

1. Mix mashed beans, breadcrumbs, and egg to form patties. 2. Pan-fry patties 4-5 minutes per side. 3. Toast buns lightly. 4. Assemble burgers with lettuce, tomato, and onion. 5. Add your favorite condiments. 6. Serve with baked sweet potato fries.

Margherita Pizza

Italian
★★★★★ (4.9/5)

Classic Neapolitan pizza with fresh mozzarella, basil, and tomato sauce. Simple perfection.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • 200ml tomato sauce
  • 200g fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Preparation

1. Preheat oven to 250°C. 2. Roll out pizza dough on floured surface. 3. Spread tomato sauce evenly. 4. Add torn mozzarella pieces. 5. Bake 10-12 minutes until crust is golden. 6. Top with fresh basil and drizzle with olive oil.

Fresh Greek Salad

Mediterranean
★★★★☆ (4.4/5)

Fresh vegetables with feta cheese and olives dressed in olive oil and herbs. A Mediterranean classic.

Ingredients

  • 2 large cucumbers, diced
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 200g feta cheese, cubed
  • 1 cup Kalamata olives
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano

Preparation

1. Combine cucumbers, tomatoes, and onion in large bowl. 2. Add feta cheese and olives. 3. Whisk together olive oil, vinegar, and oregano. 4. Pour dressing over salad and toss gently. 5. Let stand 10 minutes for flavors to meld. 6. Serve with warm pita bread.
